Lux

The Lady of Luminosity

Lux is the nerdy princess of Demacia

She is fascinated with rainbows.

This is what she looks like.

    She has 4 spells in the League of Legends.
  1. Q - Light Binding
    • Lux releases a sphere of light in a line that deals magic damage to the first two enemies hit
    • And roots them for 2 seconds
  2. W - Prismatic Barrier
    • Lux throws out her wand in a line
    • Shielding herself and allies for 3 seconds.
  3. E - Luscent Singularity
    • Lux sends an anomaly of twisted light to the target area
    • Slowing nearby enemies
  4. R - Final Spark
    • After gathering energy for 0.5 seconds
    • Lux fires a giant laser in a line that deals magic damage to all enemies hit
    • And briefly reveals them and the surrounding area
